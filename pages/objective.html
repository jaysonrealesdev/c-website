Objective

Computer Arts and Technological College serves the region and the nation by providing scientific knowledge particularly in the computer education, business and management, science and vocational-technological courses through research and interdisciplinary approaches. To this end, it aims at forming intellectually competent graduates who are morally sound and open to further growth achieve academic excellence through hard work, critical thinking and effective decision making skillful and competent in his chosen field, imbued with the right attitude through a carefully integrated curriculum in Ethics, Science and Technology, Lan guages, Business Management and Computer Education.
