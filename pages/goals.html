Goals

In consonance with the mission and objectives of the institutional consistent with the HEI's philosophy, set of goals are formulated to guide the school's operation during the next ten years. The formulation of such goals is made possible through such factors as: (a) identified educational strengths and weaknesses (b) opportunities present in the environment, and (c) institutional desires and aspiration as reflected in its vision, mission and objectives.

  • To improve the quality of CAT College students and graduates through continuous assessment of its degree programs as well as its technological and vocational courses, or careful study, design and implementation of appropriate curricular reform, new courses and other educational innovations and a more concrete faculty development program.
  • To enhance further the institution's enrolment performance by wide spreading its educational services to other areas in the Bicol Region, strengthening the marketability of the existing courses, and studying the possibility of offering other courses that will answer the educational and economic needs of the community.
  • To update and upgrade the various academic and administrative support facilities and devices in coordination with the instructional development needs of the different departments.
  • To deeply involve the institution in community and extension services making such, at the time, as avenue for teaching and personnel development.
  • To establish linkages with other institutions, business and industry making such as avenue for research.
  • To improve the administrative manpower and services, through the continuous implementation of an enhanced organizational set-up that will answer the needs of the growing student populace.
  • To continue the system of planning and evaluating to respond to the changing needs and condition of the institu- tion and the community.
  • To attain the level of an accredited higher institution of learning.

Objectives

Computer Arts and Technological College serves the region and the nation by providing scientific knowledge particularly in the computer education, business and management, science and vocational-technological courses through research and interdisciplinary approaches. To this end, it aims at forming intellectually competent graduates who

  • are morally sound and open to further growth
  • achieve academic excellence through hard work, critical thinking and effective decision making
  • skillful and competent in his chosen field, imbued with the right attitude through a carefully integrated curriculum in Ethics, Science and Technology, Languages, Business Management and Computer Education.
